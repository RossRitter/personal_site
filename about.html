Things I enjoy doing when I am not learning how to code

Reading

Being a former history professor, I spent most of my adult life reading through history books. I still read at least one book a week. I mostly read nonfiction historical books. I mostly read European history books but do not have a period I stick to.

    • Sports

    I absolutely love watching sports, and it's a passion that has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. My family loves sports, and growing up, we watched any sporting event on TV. Football, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, and I eagerly anticipate each game day during the NFL season. My favorite NFL team is the Pittsburgh Steelers. I also enjoy other sports like basketball and soccer. I used to really like college basketball; being from North Carolina, it's a way of life, but as I get older, I enjoy the NBA more. I do not have one team I follow in the NBA, but I do like to see my local Charlotte Hornets succeed. I lived in Portsmouth, England, for a year and gained an appreciation for soccer. I do love watching Premier League soccer on Saturday mornings. My poor Portsmouth FC are currently stuck in League One, but hopefully, they can return to the Premier League soon.

    Travel

    Traveling is a true passion of mine, and my heart gravitates towards the enchanting landscapes and rich histories of Western Europe. Living in England for a year was an incredible experience that allowed me to immerse myself in a new culture, indulge in the rich history, and develop a deep appreciation for the charming country. I have visited most European countries at least once, with Italy and England being my favorite. I make it to Italy at least once a year.

    Cooking

    I take pleasure in cooking, with a particular passion for Italian cuisine, especially when crafting homemade pasta. There's something deeply satisfying about the process of kneading the dough, rolling it out to perfection, and creating delicious pasta dishes from scratch, which my wife loves to eat. My best dish is pasta bolognese with handmade pappardelle noodles.

    Video Games

    I thoroughly enjoy playing video games. I like all types of games, from grand strategy games like Hearts of Iron 4 and Crusader Kings 3 to massive open-world games like Red Dead Redemption 2 and Ghost of Tsushima. The older I get, the less time I have to play.
